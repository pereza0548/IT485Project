How to find your purrfect pet

  • Browse Pets: The only requirements are a 5 digit postal code and state abbreviation. Everything else is optional. Utilize the filters to narrow down your options based on criteria of type, gender, age and size.
  • View Pet Profiles: Pet listings will show preview of their profiles from PetFinder, including name, age, breed, personality, and a photo.
  • Contact the Shelter/Owner: Use the provided contact information to inquire about and arrange a meeting with the pet.
  • Meet the Pet: Schedule a visit to meet the pet in person to ensure compatibility.
  • Adopt Your Pet: If it's a match, follow the adoption process as instructed.
  • Stay Updated: Keep checking the website for new pet listings.
